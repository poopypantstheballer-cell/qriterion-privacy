1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Qriterion mobile application (the "App"). Qriterion helps investors track dividend-paying holdings and generate stock analysis through five distinct investor lenses.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. Please read this policy carefully. By creating a Qriterion account or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

Qriterion is provided for informational purposes only. Nothing in the App constitutes financial, investment, or legal advice.

2. Who We Are

Qriterion is developed and operated by an individual developer, not a registered company.

Contact email: quentincorthouts@outlook.com

For the purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the Qriterion developer is the data controller responsible for your personal data.

3. Scope

This policy applies to all personal data processed through the Qriterion iOS application, including data stored in our backend systems.

Qriterion is intended for use by adults aged 18 and over, consistent with its focus on personal investment tracking and analysis.

4. Information We Collect

4.1 Account Information

  • Email address and password (used for account authentication)
  • Display name (optional, if you choose to set one)

4.2 Portfolio Data

  • Named portfolios you create (e.g. "Yield", "ETFs")
  • Holdings within each portfolio: ticker symbol, company name, and number of shares

Portfolio holdings are synced to our backend so they're available across app sessions. Watchlist entries (stocks you're tracking but don't own) are stored only on your device and are never sent to our servers, except that the ticker symbol itself is sent when looking up its current price or dividend data.

4.3 Investor Profile Selection

  • Which of the five investor lenses (e.g. Deep Value Hunter, Income Architect) you've selected or unlocked
  • AI-generated analysis results (score, criteria, and summary text) associated with a ticker you've analysed, cached so you can view past analyses

4.4 Usage Data

  • Number of analyses used and remaining under your membership tier, for the purpose of enforcing plan limits

4.5 Push Notification Token

  • A device-specific push token issued by Apple/Expo's push notification service, used to deliver notifications to your device

Not Collected

Qriterion does not collect your device location, contacts, camera or photo library access, biometric data (no Face ID or fingerprint data is read or stored by the App), payment card details, or advertising identifiers. Qriterion does not use any third-party analytics or crash-reporting SDKs.

5. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Providing core App functionality: authentication, portfolio tracking, and dividend calendar features
  • Generating AI-powered stock analysis through your selected investor lens
  • Enforcing membership tier limits on how many analyses you can run
  • Delivering push notifications you have opted into
  • Maintaining account security

Legal bases for processing (GDPR Article 6)

  • Contract performance: processing necessary to provide the App services you requested (e.g. tracking the portfolio you created)
  • Legitimate interest: improving App functionality and reliability
  • Consent: push notifications, and sending ticker/profile data to a third-party AI service to generate analysis

6. AI-Powered Investment Analysis

When you run an analysis on a stock, Qriterion sends the ticker symbol and your selected investor profile to a third-party AI service provider to generate the analysis text (a score, criteria breakdown, and written summary).

We do not include your name, email address, or other account identity information in this request — only the ticker symbol and the chosen investor profile are sent. The resulting analysis is then stored in our systems and associated with your account so you can view it later.

We have not independently verified the data-retention practices of the underlying AI service provider beyond what is contractually required of service providers we engage. Analyses are informational only and are not financial advice, and no automated decision with legal or similarly significant effects is made using this feature.

7. Push Notifications

Qriterion uses Expo's push notification service to deliver notifications to your device. This involves storing an Expo-issued push token associated with your account. You may disable push notifications at any time through your device settings; disabling them stops future notifications but does not delete the previously stored token until you delete your account.

8. Data Sharing and Disclosure

8.1 No Sale of Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

8.2 No Advertising Use

Your data is never used for advertising, marketing profiling, or shared with ad networks. Qriterion does not use any third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

8.3 Service Providers

We share limited data with the service providers listed in Section 10, solely to operate the App's core functionality.

9. Children and Minors

Qriterion is intended for use by adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

If we become aware that we have collected data from a user under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe someone under 18 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at quentincorthouts@outlook.com.

Depending on the infrastructure providers listed above, your data may be processed in countries outside your country of residence, including outside the European Economic Area (EEA). Where data is transferred outside the EEA, we rely on the transfer mechanisms and safeguards our service providers make available, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent protections under GDPR.

12. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active
  • Portfolio data: retained while your account is active; you may delete individual holdings or entire portfolios at any time within the App
  • Watchlist data: stored only on your device; deleted when you remove the App or clear its data
  • AI-generated analyses: retained while your account is active so you can review past results
  • Push notification token: updated on each app launch; removed on account deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Account tab within the App. Upon account deletion, your personal data is deleted from our backend within 30 days. Backup copies, if any, are purged within 90 days.

13. Your Rights (GDPR)

If GDPR applies to you, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: request deletion of your data (also available directly in the App)
  • Right to restriction: limit how we process your data
  • Right to data portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object: object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to withdraw consent: withdraw consent at any time (e.g. push notifications, AI analysis processing)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at quentincorthouts@outlook.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

14. Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) between the App and our servers
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text (handled via Supabase Auth)
  • Row-level security policies restrict access so users can only reach their own data
  • Minimal data collection — we only collect what is necessary for App functionality

  • We will notify you via push notification or in-app notice for significant changes
  • Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy

We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
